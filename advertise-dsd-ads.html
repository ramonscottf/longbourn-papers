Getting Started

How It Works

1

Contact us at supage@dsdmail.net to add DSD Ads to your Peachjar distribution

2

We use the same PDF flyer you submitted through Peachjar — no extra files needed

3

Pay $100 by check to Davis Education Foundation or credit card at dsdgive.net

4

Your flyer is emailed to all DSD employees

5

District review/approval if required for your category

Pricing

Simple, Flat-Rate Pricing

$100

Per Distribution

  • Same Peachjar flyer emailed to all DSD employees
  • Payment by check payable to Davis Education Foundation
  • Or pay by credit card at dsdgive.net (3.5% service fee applies)
Fee Exception: Any governmental entity with an established inter-local agreement or partnership with Davis School District or Davis Education Foundation is exempt from the distribution fee.

Requirements

Flyer Requirements

  • Must be PDF format
  • 8½ × 11 portrait orientation
  • No hyperlinks — use QR code or URL instead
  • Contact info and organization name clearly stated
  • Must include district disclaimer (unless Foundation or district sponsored)
“Distribution of these materials is in no way an endorsement of services, activities, and/or products by Davis School District.”

Approval

Who Needs Approval?

Not all DSD Ads require district approval, but some categories do.

Tutoring Services Contact Teaching & Learning (T&I)
Sports / Athletics Contact Healthy Lifestyles
Family Programs Contact Family Services
Curriculum-Related Contact Teaching & Learning
Safety-Related Contact Safety department
Foundation partners and district-sponsored content do not require additional approval.

Restrictions

Materials Cannot

  • Interfere with district educational objectives
  • Be obscene or libelous
  • Be religious or sectarian
  • Be contrary to district curriculum
  • Advertise products not permitted by law for minors
  • Advertise volunteer/internship/employment not sponsored by district
  • Discriminate against any person
  • Advertise during regularly scheduled school day
  • Advocate violation of school registration
