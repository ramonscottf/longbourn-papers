Most people don’t think much about paper until they touch something extraordinary. The first brush of texture between your fingers—the soft weight, the quiet strength—and suddenly you understand that not all paper is created equal.


That’s the difference of cotton paper, the beating heart of truly fine stationery. It’s not just paper. It’s a feeling, an identity, and a statement about how you communicate.


At Longbourn, every piece of paper we use begins with one choice: Crane Lettra. It’s 100% cotton, luxuriously tactile, and timeless in its craftsmanship. If you’ve ever wondered why the world’s best stationery begins with cotton, this is why.

Why Cotton Paper Matters

Paper made from wood pulp is serviceable; paper made from cotton is soulful.


Cotton fibers are longer, softer, and stronger than wood fibers. That’s what gives cotton paper its distinctive texture—the velvety smoothness that somehow still has grip. It doesn’t just look better; it feels alive beneath your pen.


Cotton paper offers:

  • Unmatched durability — It resists yellowing and tearing, lasting generations.

  • Exceptional texture — Soft, dense fibers give it that pillowy, luxurious feel.

  • Crisp impressions — It takes letterpress beautifully, leaving deep, tactile impressions that catch the light.

  • Sustainable beauty — Made from cotton linters, a byproduct of the textile industry, it’s both elegant and eco-conscious.


The result is paper that doesn’t just carry words—it honors them.

Why We Use Crane Lettra

For over 200 years, Crane has been the quiet benchmark of American papermaking.

Their Lettra line is the industry’s gold standard for letterpress and fine printing—and for good reason.


Crane Lettra is crafted from 100% recovered cotton fibers, creating a rich, naturally white sheet that’s soft to the touch yet strong enough to hold an impression without distortion. The paper absorbs ink evenly and deeply, allowing for crisp edges and lasting beauty.


Why Longbourn chooses Crane Lettra for all fine stationery:

  • It loves ink. Whether fountain pen, gel, or letterpress, Lettra takes it gracefully.

  • It feels alive. The surface is plush but structured, yielding under pressure like fabric.

  • It ages beautifully. A letter written today will look just as elegant decades from now.

  • It’s archival. 100% cotton means acid-free and made to last for generations.

 
Every Longbourn card and envelope begins with this foundation because we believe paper should have presence—something that makes the recipient pause, feel, and remember. 

How to Choose Cotton Paper That Feels Like You

The right paper isn’t about status; it’s about self-expression. Think of your stationery as an extension of your personality—your tone, your rhythm, your texture.


When selecting your perfect cotton paper, consider:

  • Color tone: Warm ivory conveys tradition; bright white feels crisp and modern.

  • Finish: Smooth papers suit fountain pens and minimal designs; textured stocks pair well with letterpress and classic monograms.

  • Weight: A heavier paper feels substantial and luxurious, ideal for formal correspondence. A lighter stock feels more intimate and conversational.

  • Format: Folded cards lend ceremony; flat cards invite brevity and charm.

 
When you find the right combination, the paper itself becomes part of your voice.

The Emotional Texture of Fine Stationery

Holding a piece of fine stationery—especially Crane Lettra—is a sensory experience. It slows you down. You can’t rush handwriting when the paper asks for grace.


Cotton paper absorbs ink in a way that feels almost alive. The letters sink slightly into the surface, becoming part of it. The tactile impression of each word turns writing into art.


That’s what makes fine stationery different:



  • It’s not disposable; it’s memorable.

  • It makes writing a pleasure, not a task.

  • It transforms communication into connection.


 
In a world of instant messages, the slowness of fine stationery isn’t an inconvenience—it’s a luxury.

Why 100% Cotton Is the Standard of Elegance

 
Some papers blend cotton with wood pulp or synthetic fibers to cut costs, but pure cotton remains unmatched in quality. It’s the choice of museums, artists, and collectors for a reason.
Here’s why 100% cotton paper reigns supreme: 


  • Strength and softness: Long fibers make it both supple and durable.

  • Color purity: Cotton naturally yields a soft, luminous white that needs no bleaching.

  • Environmental responsibility: Cotton paper often repurposes fibers from the garment industry—sustainability through craftsmanship.

  • Timeless performance: It withstands ink, embossing, and time better than any other material.

 
When you write on 100% cotton paper, you’re not just communicating—you’re continuing a tradition that began centuries ago, when permanence mattered.


At Longbourn, Paper Is Personality

Choosing Crane Lettra is our promise: that your words deserve a worthy home. Every note card, letter sheet, and invitation we create begins with this philosophy. We believe that in an age of fleeting communication, the right paper can make your message unforgettable.


When someone opens an envelope from Longbourn, they feel the weight of it—the quiet confidence, the softness of texture, the whisper of permanence. That’s what 100% cotton paper does. It carries your voice long after the ink dries.

What kind of products does Longbourn offer?

Longbourn is a curated stationery store offering timeless, ready-to-purchase fine stationery, note cards, letter sets, and writing accessories. Every piece is crafted from 100% cotton Crane Lettra paper — chosen for its elegance, durability, and unmistakable texture.

Why is Crane Lettra cotton paper used for all Longbourn stationery?

Crane Lettra is widely regarded as the finest cotton paper in the world. Made from 100% cotton, it’s smooth yet substantial, takes ink beautifully, and endures for generations. It’s the foundation of all Longbourn stationery because it captures both the beauty and permanence of handwritten words.

What makes Longbourn different from other stationery stores?

Longbourn focuses on creating a cohesive experience — modern design with traditional craftsmanship. Our pieces are meant to be used, displayed, and cherished. When you shop at our stationery store, you’re not just buying paper; you’re investing in something that helps preserve the art of human connection.

Where can I buy Longbourn products?

All Longbourn stationery is available directly through our online stationery store. Every collection ships beautifully packaged and ready for gifting or personal use.

What’s the best way to care for fine stationery?

Store your stationery flat, in a cool, dry space away from sunlight. Cotton paper loves gentle conditions — it doesn’t yellow or fade easily, but proper care ensures it stays crisp and elegant for years.

Is your stationery environmentally friendly?

Yes. Our paper is made from 100% recovered cotton fibers, a byproduct of the textile industry. By using Crane Lettra, Longbourn celebrates sustainability through craftsmanship — beauty without compromise.
