The Craft

Printed One Sheet at a Time

Our Story

Born in Bath, Made in Salt Lake City

The idea for Longbourn Papers was born during six months living in Bath, England — Jane Austen’s city, where the written word still carries weight. Named for the Bennet family estate in Pride and Prejudice, our stationery carries that same spirit of intentional correspondence.
