Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

When you visit longbournpapers.com, we may collect the following information:

  • Order information: Name, email address, shipping address, and payment details (processed securely by Shopify)
  • Contact form submissions: Name, email, and message content
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Email address
  • Usage data: Pages visited, browser type, and general location (collected anonymously via Cloudflare Web Analytics)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate about your order status
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send our newsletter (only if you subscribe)
  • Improve our website and products

How We Protect Your Information

Your personal information is stored securely. Payment processing is handled entirely by Shopify, which is PCI DSS compliant. We never store credit card information on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Shopify: E-commerce platform and payment processing
  • Cloudflare: Website hosting and analytics
  • Resend: Email communications

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for essential site functionality (cart persistence). We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at hello@longbournpapers.com.
