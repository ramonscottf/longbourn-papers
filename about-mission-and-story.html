How We Steward Gifts

We are committed to responsible stewardship, clear reporting, and partnerships that deliver measurable results. Every dollar raised is directed with intention, and every program is evaluated for the difference it makes in student lives.

  • Transparent reporting: Donors receive clear, honest updates on how their gifts are being used and the outcomes they produce.
  • Measurable results: We partner with district leaders and community organizations to track outcomes, refine programs, and ensure lasting impact.
  • Responsible growth: We expand programs deliberately—growing only as fast as community investment allows us to do so well.
  • Partnership-driven: Every initiative is built on collaboration between donors, educators, families, and community partners.

Our Story

Since 1982, Davis Education Foundation has connected community generosity with student need. What began as a small effort to support local schools has grown into a comprehensive foundation serving an entire county—driven by the belief that every student deserves a fair chance to learn, grow, and succeed.

Years of Service

Over four decades, the foundation has evolved alongside the community it serves. Today, DEF funds five impact areas that address learning, belonging, and student wellbeing from early childhood through graduation and beyond. From books in the hands of preschoolers to scholarships for graduating seniors, from athletic access to teen wellness support—every initiative reflects the same founding commitment: that when a community invests in its students, everyone benefits.

The work ahead is shaped by the same values that have guided us from the start: listen to educators, respond to need, steward gifts with care, and never lose sight of the students at the center of it all.

Why Your Gift Matters
