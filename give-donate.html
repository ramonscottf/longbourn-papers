How It Works

Give Through DSD Give

DSD Give (dsdgive.net) is Davis School District's secure online giving portal. Donors can log in with their district username and password, or give as a guest—no account required. All donations made through DSD Give to the Davis Education Foundation go directly to supporting Foundation programs.

How to Donate

Visit dsdgive.net

Go to dsdgive.net and log in or continue as a guest.

Choose the Foundation

Select "Davis Education Foundation" as the recipient of your gift.

Enter Your Gift Amount

Choose any amount that works for you. Every dollar makes a difference.

Complete Payment

Finish your donation with a credit or debit card.

All credit card transactions include a 3.5% service fee.
