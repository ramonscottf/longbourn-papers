Why It Matters

One Morning. One Course. A Year of Impact.

FORE Kids Golf isn’t just a scramble—it’s the Davis Education Foundation’s single highest-return fundraiser. In one morning at Oakridge Country Club, the tournament raises funds that go directly into programs serving students across Davis County.

The format is simple: four-person teams compete in an 18-hole scramble, with on-course contests, food trucks, and a prize drawing to close the day. But what makes it work is the partnership model behind it. Local businesses sponsor holes, carts, contests, and title-level packages—and every dollar raised flows into Cash for Classrooms grants, teacher innovation funding, and student support programs.

The event sells out regularly, bringing together business leaders, educators, and community supporters who share a belief that every student in Davis County deserves to start the school year with what they need to succeed.

FORE Kids Golf is a morning that reminds people why community investment matters. You show up to play golf and you leave knowing your team just helped a student start the year with shoes, a backpack, and the confidence to walk into school ready.
Kara Toone, Executive Director

Where Your Dollars Go

Every sponsorship, every player registration, and every prize ticket purchased at FORE Kids Golf flows directly into programs that serve students. There is a direct line from your tee box to a student starting the school year equipped and supported.

Cash for Classrooms

Annual grants awarded to schools for classroom supplies, technology, and learning materials that stretch every dollar further for educators.

Teacher & Innovation Grants

Competitive grants that empower educators to pilot creative instructional approaches, hands-on learning projects, and student engagement initiatives.

Student Supports

Direct student aid including school supplies, shoes, clothing, winter coats, and emergency resources—so no student starts the year without what they need.

Why This Works

The Case for FORE Kids Golf

FORE Kids Golf works because it connects community leaders to student impact in a way that feels personal, is professionally managed, and delivers measurable return on investment.

  • Direct line to impact: Every dollar raised goes into student-facing programs—no overhead, no ambiguity. Sponsors know exactly where their investment lands.
  • Proven, efficient model: This is one of the highest-ROI events in Davis County philanthropy—efficient, proven, and scalable.
  • Community cachet: The tournament sells out because the business community sees it as the premier opportunity to invest in local education and connect with peers who share that mission.
  • Year-round recognition: Sponsors are acknowledged at the event, in Foundation communications, and in donor reports—visibility that extends well beyond the course.

Event Day

What to Expect

From registration to the final prize drawing, FORE Kids Golf is built around community, friendly competition, and shared purpose. Here’s how the morning unfolds.

Registration & Cart Lineup — 7:30 AM

Check in, pick up your player package (3 mulligans, 6 prize tickets, and a licorice rope), and line up your cart for the shotgun start.

Carts to Holes — 8:15 AM

Head out to your assigned hole and get ready for the scramble. Meet your hole sponsors and settle in.

Shotgun Start — 8:30 AM

18-hole scramble with on-course contests: Closest to the Hole, Putting Contest, Pitching Contest, Straightest Drive, Longest Drive, and Hole in One. Enjoy a food truck at hole #10 courtesy of Resolut.

Awards & Prize Drawing

Gather for the awards ceremony, prize drawing ($5 each or 6 for $20), and player prizes provided by Hughes General Contractors. Celebrate the winning teams and the impact you’ve made for students.
