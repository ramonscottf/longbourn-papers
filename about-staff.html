Who We Are

The crew that gets excited about the “small stuff.”

We’re the crew that gets excited about the “small stuff” because we know it’s never actually small — a teacher who needs supplies by Monday, a student who needs shoes that fit, a classroom that needs a boost to make learning click.

We work every day to expand learning beyond the classroom, remove obstacles that hold students back, and get resources into the hands of educators across Davis School District.

We’re proud to be two-time Best of State Statue winners and multiple-time winners across categories, but the real win is simple: helping more kids show up, feel supported, and succeed — and helping teachers do what they do best without unnecessary barriers in the way.
