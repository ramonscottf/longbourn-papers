Where the Funding Goes

Gifts designated to Student Supports fund the programs that keep students steady. That includes:

  • Homeless assistance for students without stable housing
  • Basic needs including food, clothing, and hygiene essentials
  • Holiday assistance through Sub for Santa and related programs
  • Safe spaces through Teen Centers and the Teen Living Center
  • Back-to-school readiness through Child Spree and Kids’ Closet

Over $1,200,000 has been distributed for homeless assistance, basic needs, food, clothing, and holiday assistance—reaching 5,500 at-risk students who need stability to stay focused on learning.
