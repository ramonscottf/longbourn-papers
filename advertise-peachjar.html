Distribution Categories

Who Can Distribute

Peachjar distribution is managed through the Davis Education Foundation. The approval process, fees, and requirements depend on the type of organization requesting distribution.

District & Schools

Must be district or school sponsored. Funds go through DSD Give. No disclaimer needed.

Approval: Principal or Department Head

Upload: Foundation uploads

Fees: No Peachjar or district fees

Foundation Partners

Review with Foundation Director. Must have Foundation logo or state it is for Davis Education Foundation.

Upload: Foundation uploads

Fees: No Peachjar or district fees

City Rec / City Events

City Rec allowed 2 free distributions per year (once per semester for sports signup or other). Free city events reviewed and approved by DEF (must say “Free” on flyer). Disclaimer required.

Upload: City uploads, DEF emails Peachjar permission

Fees: Free (limited distributions)

Businesses

District review required if applicable (Tutoring: T&I, Sports: Healthy Lifestyles, Family Programs: Family Services). Free events reviewed on a flyer-to-flyer basis. Disclaimer required.

Upload: Business uploads and pays Peachjar fees

Payment: Credit card or check

Government

District review by Risk Management or other district department. When possible, add disclaimer. Sometimes it is a government mandatory request.

Upload: Government uploads, DEF approves

Guidelines

Flyer Requirements

Formatting

  • 8½ × 11 portrait PDF
  • No hyperlinks — use QR code or URL
  • Contact info and organization clearly stated
  • Non-district/non-Foundation flyers must include disclaimer
Required Disclaimer: “Distribution of these materials is in no way an endorsement of services, activities, and/or products by Davis School District.”

Materials Cannot

  • Interfere with district educational objectives
  • Be obscene or libelous in nature
  • Be religious or sectarian in nature
  • Be contrary to district curriculum
  • Advertise products or services not permitted by law for minors
  • Advertise or solicit volunteer, internship, or employment opportunities not sponsored by the district
  • Discriminate against any person in any way
  • Advertise activities or services during regularly scheduled school day
  • Advocate the violation of school registration
