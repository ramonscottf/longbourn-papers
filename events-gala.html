More Than a Movie Night

The Annual Gala is the Foundation's flagship fundraiser, bringing together our community to support programs that make a real difference. Every dollar raised funds classroom grants, teacher recognition, student scholarships, and enrichment programs across Davis School District.

Funding What Matters

Gala proceeds fund classroom innovation grants, literacy programs, and student support services throughout the district.
