Partnership Options

We work with businesses of all sizes to structure partnerships that align with your goals and deliver measurable outcomes for students. Every partnership is tailored, transparent, and built for impact.

  • Impact area investment — Support an impact lane such as Literacy, Scholarships, STEM, or Mental Health and see the direct results of your commitment.
  • Event sponsorship — Gain visibility and community goodwill through signature events including the Gala, Fore Kids Golf Tournament, and Child Spree.
  • Multi-year commitments — Sustain program growth with predictable, long-term support that helps us plan responsibly and expand reach.
  • In-kind support and services — Contribute professional expertise, technology, supplies, or other resources that directly benefit students and educators.

Stewardship and Reporting

We commit to clear deliverables, proof of completion for sponsorships, and reporting through annual and program updates. Every corporate partner receives a dedicated point of contact, regular communication, and documentation of how your investment performed.

Recognition packages are structured around your preferences—from logo placement and event presence to social media features and employee engagement opportunities. We treat your partnership as a relationship, not a transaction.

Local Impact

Davis Education Foundation is the education foundation for Davis School District, the third largest district in Utah. Your partnership reaches students across 92 schools and more than 72,000 enrolled students—all within your community.

We operate with low overhead, clear impact lanes, and a commitment to measurable outcomes. Partners receive transparent reporting and direct access to leadership, making it easy to demonstrate the return on your community investment.

  • Measurable outcomes — Every program is tracked and reported, giving your team real data to share with stakeholders.
  • Community visibility — Signature events and district-wide programs put your brand in front of families, educators, and civic leaders.
  • Flexible structures — We design partnerships around your budget, timeline, and priorities—not ours.
  • Trusted stewardship — As a 501(c)(3) public charity, DEF operates with full financial transparency and board oversight.
