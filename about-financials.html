Stewardship & Donation Policy

The Davis Education Foundation abides by the policies and procedures of the Davis School District in accordance with the laws of the State of Utah. Our commitment to following these regulations ensures that all donations are handled with the highest standards of transparency and accountability.

Designated gifts are always directed as intended. Unrestricted gifts are allocated to areas of greatest need across the district, ensuring that resources reach students and teachers where they matter most.
