Scholarships

Scholarships that open doors.

We help students succeed by managing privately funded scholarships for Davis School District students. These scholarships open doors to opportunity through academic achievement, leadership, service, career and technical pathways, and tributes that honor someone’s legacy.

Apply

If you are a student looking to apply for scholarships, your first step is to connect with your school counselor.

Counselors can provide a comprehensive list of available opportunities and guide you through the application process, helping you find the awards that best align with your personal goals and interests.
