Who We Are

The philanthropic partner of Davis School District.

Founded in 1982, Davis Education Foundation is the dedicated fundraising partner supporting public education across Davis County, Utah. We connect community generosity with programs that help students thrive.

As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we raise and steward private support for initiatives that remove barriers to learning, provide enrichment opportunities, and foster classroom excellence across all 92 district schools.

Our Mission

Expanding opportunity for every student.

Davis Education Foundation raises and stewards private support to expand student opportunity and strengthen educational outcomes. Our three-fold outreach drives everything we do.

1

Remove Barriers to Learning

We fund programs that address the non-academic challenges students face, from basic needs to mental health support, so they can focus on learning.

2

Provide Educational Opportunities

From scholarships and early literacy to enrichment programs and athletics, we create pathways for students to grow, explore, and succeed.

3

Foster Classroom Excellence

Classroom grants, strategic investments, and long-term initiatives strengthen teaching and learning conditions across all 92 schools.

Serving Since 1982

Four decades of community impact.

1982

Davis Education Foundation is established as the philanthropic partner of Davis School District, creating a dedicated channel for community support of public education.

1990s

The Foundation expands its scope, launching scholarship programs and early student support initiatives that reach families across Davis County.

2000s

Community partnerships deepen as the Foundation introduces classroom grants, Teen Centers, and programs addressing barriers to learning.

2012

A pivotal year of strategic growth. Since 2012, the Foundation has invested more than $64 million in student programs, scholarships, and classroom support.

2020s

The Catalyst initiative launches, scaling high-impact strategies across all 92 schools. Early literacy programs expand through partnerships like Dolly Parton’s Imagination Library.

Today

Serving 72,000+ students annually, the Foundation continues to connect community generosity with measurable outcomes across Davis County schools.
