Who We Are

The philanthropic partner of Davis School District.

The Davis Education Foundation exists to bridge the gap between what schools can fund and what students need. We work alongside educators, families, and community partners to create access, equity, and opportunity.

1982 Founded and serving students
92 Schools supported across the district
72,000+ Students reached each year

Mission First

Everything starts with student-centered purpose.

Mission
