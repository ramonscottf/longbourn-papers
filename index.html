The Art of Letterpress

550 Years of Tradition,
One Press at a Time

Born in Gutenberg’s workshop and refined through centuries of master printers, letterpress is the art of pressing ink into paper with physical type and hand-carved plates. The result is a tactile impression—a gentle kiss between metal and cotton fiber—that no digital printer can replicate.

At Longbourn, we honor this tradition while bringing it to life for modern occasions.

From Studio to Ribbon

How Each Piece Comes to Life

Design

Every motif is hand-illustrated and refined for the letterpress medium—designed to create the deepest, most beautiful impression.

Plate & Press

Custom photopolymer plates are locked into our press. Ink is mixed by hand to achieve the perfect hue, then applied with precision.

Impression

Each sheet of cotton paper is fed individually through the press. The plate meets paper under thousands of pounds of pressure.

Finishing

Cards are die-cut, paired with coordinating envelopes, and adorned with hand-tied satin ribbon. Ready to delight.
