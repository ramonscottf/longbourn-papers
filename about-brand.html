Our Logo

Our logo is more than just a graphic — it’s a visual reflection of our mission to connect students with the resources they need to thrive.

The central design element forms the shape of a D for Davis, but look closer and you’ll see more: one figure reaching out, arms extended in care, wrapping around another. This image represents connection, support, and the powerful relationships that form the heart of education.

Guidelines

Usage Rules

Consistency protects the brand. Follow these guidelines whenever using our logo and identity assets.

Do

  • Use the dark logo on white or light backgrounds
  • Use the light logo on dark backgrounds (navy, black, photos)
  • Ensure sufficient contrast so the logo is clear and readable
  • Use the full logo whenever possible
  • Reserve the emblem for profile images and compact spaces
  • Use the D icon only as a favicon, badge, or small digital element

Don’t

  • Put the dark logo on dark backgrounds
  • Put the white logo on light backgrounds
  • Use drop shadows, gradients, or alter the colors
  • Stretch, squish, or crop the logo
  • Add effects like bevels, outlines, or glows
  • Rotate or skew the logo at an angle
