The Awards

Five Programs, One Mission: Honoring Great Teaching

Davis Education Foundation runs five distinct teacher recognition programs—each designed to celebrate a different dimension of excellent teaching, from career-spanning dedication to day-one classroom readiness. Together, they create a culture where educators feel seen, valued, and supported at every stage.

Teacher of the Year

District Flagship Award

The district’s flagship recognition. One educator is selected annually as the exemplar of classroom impact, instructional leadership, and community influence. Surprise ceremonies at the winner’s school create high-emotion moments that resonate across the district.

Prize: One-year vehicle lease + maintenance + insurance

Sponsors: Young Automotive Group / Young Kia · Goldenwest Credit Union

Flagship Award Vehicle Lease Surprise Ceremony

Heidi Martin — Mighty Heart Educator of Excellence

$10,000 Award

Named for Heidi Ann Martin, this award recognizes an educator who models resilience, compassion, and human connection. It honors teachers who move hearts and lives as much as test scores—the ones who stay late, show up early, and make every child feel they matter.

Prize: $10,000 to the winner’s school

Sponsors: Anonymous Founding Donor · Best of Davis Partners (Chevron, Hogan Construction)

$10,000 Prize School Discretion Best of Davis

Watch the story behind the Heidi Martin Mighty Heart Award—how it honors educators who model resilience, compassion, and human connection, and how it impacts their schools and communities.

New Teacher Support & Outstanding New Educator

Day-One Investment

Celebrates early-career teachers through two tracks: the New Teacher Orientation / Classroom Wallet program gives every new-to-district teacher a $100 starter grant, and the Outstanding New Educator recognition spotlights exceptional first-year teachers with gift baskets and public recognition.

Prize: $100 Classroom Wallet per teacher (300+ teachers) + gift basket for Outstanding New Educator

Sponsors: Mountain America Credit Union · Young Caring for Our Young · Lifetime Products · GPS Capital Markets · Tanner Clinic

300+ Teachers $100 Each Day-One Impact

Lifetime Achievement — America First

Career Legacy Award

Recognizes educators whose careers span decades—mentorship, leadership, and steady service across generations of students. This award celebrates the lifetime dedication that shapes a school’s culture long after the honoree retires.

Prize: Cash honorarium + in-kind gifts

Naming Sponsor: America First Credit Union

Career Legacy Mentorship America First

Excellence in Education — Murdock Family Foundation

Year-Long Recognition

A year-long, data-driven recognition program highlighting elementary teachers with exceptional reading-growth outcomes, instructional innovation, and leadership. Monthly recognition tied to measurable student outcomes creates sustained motivation and visibility.

Prize: Monthly and yearly recognition (Murdock Family Foundation)

Sponsor: Murdock Family Foundation
